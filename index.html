A bit about me...

I was born in 1997 in an Upper Silesian city - Katowice. Computers became object of curiosity to me since I played Quake 2 for the first time in my life (thanks ID Software)... In the beggining I discovered simple art of VBScripts. I was 10 years old back then. I started with creating loops of message boxes to perform simple pranks on my friends and family (and yes - it was a thing back then). It became boring quickly, so I wanted to advance with something more complex. Someday I accidentaly came on something that is called a computer virus today. Believe me or not - the first beep of my AV started the fear that scared me the most as a kid, computer viruses... Soon after that, my fear was replaced by curiosity. I could not stop reading about viruses and how they work (I even tried some reverse engineering). I ended up helping people removing their infections on polish forums when I was 12 (Anybody remembers W32/Virut or Sality? I do). That's how my fascination for security and software engineering started.
Since then I am continously trying to learn new technologies and develop myself.
